Basic Information

Benjamin Clarence Phillips Jr. (born June 24, 1998), known professionally as Autumn (stylized as Autumn!), is an American rapper and record producer. He initially started as a producer under the name TrapGodBenji. He is best known for popularizing the pluggnb subgenre alongside fellow artists Summrs and Kankan

Recent Albums: Antagonist!,Golden Child, Chapter 3,Midnight Club,Midnight Club: Dub Edition
